TYPE OF SUSSY BAKA



SNEAKY WHITE COW

A white cow is a type of cattle that is white in color with black points. They are a traditional British breed and are naturally polled dual-purpose cattle. White cattle were highly regarded in Britain and Ireland in very early times, and herds of white cattle were kept as ornamental and sporting animals in enclosed parks for many centuries. White cow breeds can be beef cattle or dual-purpose cattle that provide milk too.

AGGRESSIVE BLACK COW

A black cow is any cow that has a black coat color. Black cows are not a separate breed of cow, but rather a variation within different breeds of cattle. Some of the most popular breeds that produce black cows are Angus, Hereford, and Holstein. Black cows may also have other colors on their coat s, such as white or red.
